About Us

Vidya means Education and Vidya Ashram is a humble effort to create a pious place where students are taught never to miss their targets Vidya Ashram is dedicated to bring out the best in students so that they come out with flying colours in competitive examinations.

WHY CHOOSE US

Classes in School premises

Classes for the students enrolled in this arrangement will be conducted within the school premises, also by the faculties of Vidya Ashram. This will not only save the valuable time of the student but also enable them in adjustment and harmony between their approach of preparation for their board as well as the competitive examination. The student's major exams at school are taken into consideration while scheduling classes

Experienced Faculty.

Our strength lies in its unmatched quality of teaching methodologies and a team of renowned Faculty members of National repute. The Faculty team comprises of experienced best brains brought together from various educational hubs.

Pattern Proof Course Design.

We have designed the course wherein the initial phase aims at strengthening the basics and fundamental concepts of the subject. Thereafter, higher level concepts are taught that strengthens the analytical ability, concept interpretation skills and problem solving skills of the students. The entire course plan for the session [Syllabus division, Sequencing of topics, Test-dates and Result preparation dates] are done in advance. We give utmost importance to "Timely Completion of Course" without compromising on the Syllabus. We have the most Efficient Course Tracking Team. The Course is comprehensively designed in a way that a Student gets equipped to perform well in BOARD, Competitive and Scholarship Examinations.
